My dearest,

Six years ago, you walked into my world and rearranged everything — gently, completely, beautifully. I never knew that love could feel so much like coming home.

You are my favourite part of every single day. The way your eyes light up over small things. The sound of your laughter in the morning. The way you reach for my hand without thinking about it.

Thank you for choosing me, again and again, in every ordinary and extraordinary moment. Thank you for being the person I get to grow with, grow old with, and grow more in love with every day.

Here's to us — to all the years we've woven together, and all the ones still ahead.

Forever yours, — Your Love ♡
